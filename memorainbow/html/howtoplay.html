MEMORY GAME - RAINBOW EDITION

What is MemoRainbow?



Click on the rainbow tiles to flip them around
and reveal their backside image.

After three rainbow tiles are clicked on and flipped,
they flip back around and show their rainbow coated backs.


Unless...

...the three tiles are equal.


Oh, and watch out for Street Pete!



If you find all the Street Pete tiles...


...he'll flip back around all your completed tile groups!!!


Try remembering Street Pete's location, and avoid clicking on him again.
He stays under the same tiles until the game is restarted or completed.


GOOD LUCK!!!
